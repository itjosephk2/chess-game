        Chess Rules

        Objective: The goal is to checkmate your opponent's king.

        • Pawn: Moves forward 1 square (2 on first move), captures diagonally.
        • Rook: Moves horizontally or vertically any number of squares.
        • Knight: Moves in an “L” shape. Can jump over pieces.
        • Bishop: Moves diagonally any number of squares.
        • Queen: Combines rook and bishop movements.
        • King: Moves 1 square in any direction.

        Special Moves:

        • Castling: King and rook move at the same time under specific conditions.
        • En Passant: Special pawn capture under certain conditions.
        • Promotion: When a pawn reaches the far end, it becomes another piece.

        Winning: You win by checkmating the opponent’s king or if they resign.

        Checkmate!

        White wins!

        Draw
        The game ended in a stalemate. It's a draw!
